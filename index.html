matcha brownies

prep time: 10 minutes

cook time: 25 minutes

ingredients:

  • 300g white chocolate
  • 1/2 cup melted butter
  • 3tbsp matcha powder
  • 1/3 cup white sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • salt
  • 2/3 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 tsp baking powder

instructions:

  1. preheat oven to 180C
  2. line a baking pan with parchment paper
  3. melt 180g white chocolate and add melted butter
  4. add the matcha powder and sugar to the mixture and stir well
  5. add the eggs and mix
  6. add the flour, salt, and baking powder until everything is well combined
  7. chop the remaining white chocolate and mix it in the batter
  8. pour into the baking tray and bake for 25 minutes
  9. cool and serve

chocolate chip cookies


prep time: 15 minutes

cook time: 15 minutes

ingredients:

  • 1/2 cup room temp butter
  • 1 cup brown sugar
  • 1/3 cup white sugar
  • 1 egg
  • 2 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 2 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 tsp baking powder
  • chocolate chunks

instructions:

  1. preheat oven to 180C
  2. line a baking tray with parchment paper
  3. in a large bowl, mix the butter with the brown and white sugar
  4. add the egg, and the vanilla extract
  5. add the flour, salt, and baking powder until everything is well combined
  6. mix in the chocolate chunks
  7. roll the dough into balls and place on the lined tray
  8. bake for 12-15 minutes
  9. serve

matcha cookies


prep time: 15 minutes

cook time: 15 minutes

ingredients:

  • 1/2 cup melted butter
  • 1/2 cup brown sugar
  • 1/4 cup white sugar
  • 1 egg
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • salt
  • 1 1/4 all-purpose flour
  • 1 tbsp cornstarch
  • 1/2 tsp baking soda
  • 1 tbsp matcha powder
  • white chocolate chunks

instructions:

  1. preheat oven to 180C
  2. line a baking tray with parchment paper
  3. in a large bowl, mix the melted butter with the brown and white sugar
  4. add the egg, and the vanilla extract
  5. add the flour, salt, baking soda, cornstarch, and matcha powder until everything is well combined
  6. mix in the white chocolate chunks
  7. roll the dough into balls and place on the lined tray
  8. bake for 12-15 minutes
  9. serve
